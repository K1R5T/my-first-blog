My Second Post

You can find the Django girls tutorial at tutorial.djangogirls.org if you want to give this a go. It will take you step by step through everything, from downloading each part needed to what you need to type and the finished project. I found this useful, if I was to do the hard work alone with no help I would have been googling for hours before even starting a download because I wouldn't know what to do, but Django girls talk you through all of that.
